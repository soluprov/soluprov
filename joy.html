Joy Smith

Director of Projects
+16613684763

Our ability to safely deliver quality projects to our clients on time and budget determines our value as a consultant. As Director of Projects, Joy advises our Project Managers on cost estimating, project scheduling, resource allocation, and project management needs to make sure our projects stay on track. Drawing on 23 years of experience managing large municipal, transportation, and commercial development projects, Joy has honed her project controls expertise to assess design, procurement and construction processes. She works to minimize risk by helping our Project Managers track and measure performance, control cost and schedule, predict project outcomes and effectively communicate project status to clients. Joy holds a BS in Civil Engineering from the University of Texas
