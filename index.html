COVID-19 Planning and Response

Baccuitecture's primary concerns during the COVID-19 pandemic are preventing the spread of the virus, keeping our employees safe, and delivering solutions for our clients. Our non-field employees are working remotely as we continue to meet our clients’ project schedule and deliverable commitments. Our field staff continue to provide construction observation and survey services on critical infrastructure projects.

Baccuitecture understands our clients may need additional support moving their projects forward during this challenging time. Please let us know how we can help.

Some clients have struggled with maintaining team communications. Examples of web-based communication services we have provided to support clients include:
• Whereby.com – This is an extremely simple and straightforward service. However, functionality is limited to a small number of participants without any two-way screen share.

Please reach out to your Baccuitecture's contact, or contact Rent Everett, President and CEO via +12015283580 if Baccuitecture can assist you with project communications or provide any additional support services at this time.
